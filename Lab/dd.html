Term and Definition List

Abject
Extremely bad, unpleasant, and degrading; completely without pride or dignity.
Conduit
A channel or pipe for conveying water or other fluids; a means of transmitting information or distributing resources.
Demagogue
A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument.
